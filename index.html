Introduction

The Industrial Revolution was a time of big changes and inventions. It was the start of new machines, factories, and ways of working. Life was very different back then, with people moving from farms to cities and working hard in noisy factories. It was a period full of energy and new ideas.

How It Was Like & Looked

Back then, everything looked very busy and noisy. The streets were filled with smoke from factories and the sounds of machines clanking all day long. People wore simple clothes and worked hard from early morning till evening. The buildings were mostly made of brick with tall chimneys, and the air often smelled like coal and steam. It was a time of excitement and challenge, with a mix of old and new things everywhere.
